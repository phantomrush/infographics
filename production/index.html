Competitiveness Mindset Study

This study under the aegis of IIT Gandhinagar seeks to identify factors that can boost India's GDP and job creation by looking at the factors that affect Indians' competitiveness mindset. It has ranked the importance of 25 behavioral and cultural attitudes/issues for competitiveness mindset in general and then provided a score on India's standing for each of the issues.
